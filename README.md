#Welcome to Sonix! 

*Your personal, beautifully crafted music streaming simulator for iOS.* 

---

##  What is Sonix?

**Sonix** is a native iOS app written in **Swift** that brings the magic of a digital music streaming platform right to your fingertips! It was built with love to showcase a gorgeous user interface while dynamically pulling real-world music data. 

Whether you want to discover new genres, read up on your favorite artists, or just browse through stunning album covers, Sonix offers a smooth, immersive experience that feels exactly like a premium streaming app.

---

##  Awesome Features

*    **Discover the Magic:** Dive into a massive catalog of artists, trending genres, and new album releases.
*    **Artist Spotlights:** Get up close and personal with comprehensive artist bios, top tracks, and discographies.
*    **Sleek UI:** A modern, beautiful, and intuitive design that makes browsing music a joy.

---

##  What's Under the Hood?

Sonix is proudly built for the iOS ecosystem. To make the music data feel alive, it teams up with two amazing APIs:

*    **[Deezer API](https://developers.deezer.com/api)
*    **[Last.fm API](https://www.last.fm/api)
